Privacy Policy

PlayerLog is built for families who want to track their kids' sports stats — nothing more.

Data We Collect

PlayerLog stores the data you enter: player names, game dates, opponents, and sports statistics. This data is saved to your iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service and syncs across your devices.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to power the app. We do not analyze it, share it, sell it, or use it for advertising.

iCloud & CloudKit

PlayerLog uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across devices signed into the same iCloud account. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud container and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Analytics or usage data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Third-party SDKs or tracking tools
  • Location data
  • Data from children beyond what you manually enter in the app

Children's Privacy

PlayerLog is designed to track youth sports stats entered by parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children directly.

Data Deletion

To delete your data, delete the app. Your iCloud data can be managed in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud on your device.
